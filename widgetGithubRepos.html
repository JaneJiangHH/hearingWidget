For your information

There are 10 different hearing levels, from 1 to 10. After each hearing test, the user will be given a result.

1-3 means the person may experience some hearing issues, it is better to have a check at some professional clinic.
4-7 means the hearing level is OK, but still need to pay some attention to hearing health.
8 and onwards means the person is totally fine with hearing. There is no need to worry.

For each hearing test, there are two rounds of tests in succession. The user has to answer at least one round correctly, then can he/she moves on to next hearing level, or back to previous hearing level.
